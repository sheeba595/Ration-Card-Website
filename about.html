About the Ration Card Portal

The Ration Card Portal is an initiative by the Department of Food and Civil Supplies to streamline the process of applying for, tracking, and managing ration cards. This portal ains to provide a user-friendlly and efficient platform for citizens to access essential services related to their ration cards.

Purpose

The primary purpose of this portal is to simplify the application process for new ration cards, allow users to track the status of their applications in real-time, and provide a convenient way to manage existing ration cards. It also serves as a central hub for information and updates regarding ration card policies and procedures.

Responsible Department

This portal is managed and maintained by the Department of Food and Civil Supplies, Government of India. The department is responsible for ensuring the efficient distribution of essential commodities to eligible citizens through the Public Distribution System (PDS)
